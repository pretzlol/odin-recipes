Alicia's favorite fries recipe!

Description

Seasoned to bring you a memorable taste experience. They're crispy, crunchy and delicious, making them ideal for serving with burgers or by themselves. One of a kind flavor with just the right amount of seasoning

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Deep fryer
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Open fry package.
  2. Empty fries into deep fryer.
  3. Fry for 2 minutes 30 seconds.
  4. Pull fries out of deep fryer.
  5. Final cook them for 30 seconds when ready to serve to someone.
  6. Serve when ready!
