Aaron's Ramen Recipe

Description

A beef maruchan ramen dish. Very light colored noodles, with an addicting taste. The ramen will be hard whenever you pull them out of the microwave if done correctly. However, you let the ramen sit in hot water, and they will become squishy like regular noodles.

Ingredients

    Maruchan Ramen package Maruchan Ramen seasoning packet Water to where the ramen can be submerged

Steps

    Grab a bowl. Grab a Maruchan Ramen package. Open the package. Empty the package into the bowl. Open seasoning packet. Empty seasoning packet onto ramen. Fill the bowl with water until you can submerge the ramen. Place the bowl into a microwave. Microwave for two minutes. Take the bowl out of the microwave. Let the ramen sit in water for 1-2 minutes. Pour the water out when noodles are squishy enough for you. Grab a fork and enjoy!
